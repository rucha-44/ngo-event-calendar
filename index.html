About us

Vision

To help build a more influential, equal and socially conscious society

Mission

To drive social change by fostering an environment where young adults and children learn, lead, and thrive

Values

Bhumi believes that the culture of our organisation is built over the years, because of the deep rooted value system that we believe in. These values are what we aspire to translate in all our stakeholders and our work

Collaboration

Coming together of people and organisations for a shared purpose leading to the greater good. We strongly believe together we can do more for a better society

Events

Bhumi conducts various events throughout the year. The most prominent categories of the events/workshops conducted at Bhumi are listed below.

Environment

We conduct various tree plantation drives and clean-up drives.

Animals

We make sure to try to shelter and tend to as many animals as possible.

Children

We conduct educational drives for under-privileged children.

Senior Citizens

We conduct activities to maintain health and wellbeing of senior citizens.

Projects

At Bhumi, we focus on three main things. One is educating under-privileged children at shelter homes or community centers on all school subjects. Another is our social work for the environment and all categories of people and animals. The last is doing fundraisers, planning competitions and organising educational events for children.
